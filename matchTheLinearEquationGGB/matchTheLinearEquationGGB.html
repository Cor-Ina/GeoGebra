Review: Write an Equation in Slope-Intercept Form

                    
To write an equation in the form y = mx + b we need m 
                        and b. 

                        

                    
     m is the slope, or the "rise over run" between two points on the line.
                    
     b is the y-intercept (the point where the line crosses the y axis).
